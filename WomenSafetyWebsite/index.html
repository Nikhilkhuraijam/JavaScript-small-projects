Essential Safety Tips

🔐

Strong Passwords

Use passwords with mix of letters, numbers and symbols. Never share passwords. Use different passwords for different accounts.

👤

Privacy Settings

Make your social media private. Control who can see your posts, photos and personal information. Review settings regularly.

🚫

Stranger Danger

Never trust strangers online. Be careful with personal information. Verify identities before sharing details.

🔗

Avoid Suspicious Links

Don't click unknown links. Check URLs before clicking. Be wary of shortened links from unknown sources.

💬

Think Before Sharing

Once posted, it's permanent. Think about who can see your content. Avoid sharing location or schedules.

📱

Device Safety

Keep software updated. Use antivirus protection. Never leave devices unattended. Lock your screen.

🎥

Camera & Mic Safety

Cover your webcam. Mute microphone when not in use. Be cautious in video calls with strangers.

⚠️

Recognize Harassment

Unwanted messages, threats, or intimidation is harassment. Document everything. Block and report. Don't respond to trolls.
